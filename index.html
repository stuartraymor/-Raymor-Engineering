About Me

Hi, I'm Stuart Raymor, a fourth-generation machinist with a lifelong passion for design, engineering, and manufacturing. Currently, I work as a Machinist III in the maintenance department at Amway, where I troubleshoot, design, prototype, and optimize complex machinery and manufacturing systems.

With extensive experience in CNC machining, Mastercam programming, SolidWorks modeling, and injection molding, I specialize in creating efficient solutions for tooling, automation, and large-scale manufacturing processes. I’m also pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Product Design Engineering at Ferris University, building on my Associate’s degree in Tooling and Manufacturing.

From managing multi-million-dollar tooling projects to developing custom automation systems, I thrive in fast-paced environments where precision and innovation are key. My diverse background includes working with Class A finishes for automotive components, reverse engineering, Lean Manufacturing implementation, and hands-on collaboration with engineers and production teams.

Outside of work, I run Raymor Machining, offering specialized machining, design, and engineering services. I’m always looking for opportunities to apply my skills, solve complex problems, and build lasting professional relationships.
